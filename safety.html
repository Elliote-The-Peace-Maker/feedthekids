Travel safely in Mzansi

South Africa is a beautiful country, but like any major tourist destination, it pays to stay vigilant. Here are our top tips for a safe trip:

  • Transport Safety: Always use verified transport partners booked through our site. If using e-hailing apps, verify the license plate and driver name before getting in.
  • Payment Scams: Never wire money or pay via EFT directly to a property owner outside of the STA Travel platform.
  • Situational Awareness: Avoid flashing expensive cameras, jewelry, or large amounts of cash in busy public areas or unfamiliar neighborhoods.
  • Route Planning: If driving a rental car, plan your route before leaving. Stick to major highways and avoid driving in unfamiliar rural areas at night.

Report a Safety Issue

If you felt unsafe at a property, experienced an issue with a transport provider, or suspect a scam, please report it immediately. Our Trust & Safety team will investigate.
